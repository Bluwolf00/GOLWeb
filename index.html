Guerrillas Of Liberation

est. 2003

An international Arma community with over 20 years of experience!

Weekly Operations

We conduct operations every Sunday night at 5pm GMT and Thursday night at 7pm GMT.
Our missions are created in-house using our own framework built over decades of work and our missions also put an emphasis on realism, action and narrative.

Dedicated Servers

Our dedicated game and TeamSpeak servers are available 24 hours a day for our members to have a chat or play some games together. The use of our game servers for games other than ArmA is permitted for any interested members.

Modifications

We use various mods from the community including ACE and TFAR during our operations to enhance the experience. We have a large customised framework for our Arma 3 missions that enhance gameplay.

Check out our Modlist >

Recruitment Period

For any new member joining us, they will initially join as a recruit. After completing 2 operations of each type (Thursday & Sunday), they are promoted to a full time member starting at private.

Non-Restrictive Ranks

The main purpose of ranks are to reflect a member's experience and knowledge, nothing more. Everyone has a voice, regardless of rank.

No One Faction

We utilise all kinds of factions and nations that the Arma platform has to offer. Playing the same faction over and over can be come tedious, so we offer a good handful such as: US, UK, Germany, Latvia, Finland, Sweden, Norway, Czech, RU, as well as many independent factions.

No One Composition

As well as a variety of factions. We train for all kinds of compositions, such as, Motorised, Mechanised, Special Forces, Air Assault and Basic Infantry Squads.

Dedicated Training

In order for our members to be familiar with both technical and tactical knowledge, we have dedicated trainings on a vaariety of subjects.

International Unit

Despite the fact that over half of our member base is from the UK. We still have a large portion of members that come from all over europe and further.

Basic ACE Medical

Tired of watching a black screen for 15 minutes because you got ArmA'd? Well we certainly are. Which is why we've cranked the dial back on the the medical system, making it simpler and easier to get back into the fight.

Editing & Extras

We also host editing sessions for those interested in exploring ArmA 3's 3DEN editor. For missions made that are of good quality, we'll also add them to our rotation!
